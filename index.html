AETHER MATHEMATICS OPEN

灵息杯数学竞赛，创办于2026年，由学生团体自主创办。目前考核的范围仅在中国一般初中所教授的内容中。总部设于中国桂林，仅在此开放针对中小学生的线下赛事，同时对全球人类开放线上赛事。

The Aether Mathematics Open (AMO), established in 2026, is a mathematics competition founded by a student organization. The scope of assessment is currently limited to the curriculum of ordinary junior secondary schools in China. Headquartered in Guilin, China, the competition offers offline events exclusively for primary and secondary school students in this locality, while online events are open to all participants worldwide.

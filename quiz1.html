Scoring: Problems 1-4: 10 points each. Problem 5: 15 points per sub-problem. Total: 70 points

第1题

求所有正整数 $n$，使得 $n^2 + 3n + 2$ 为完全平方数。

Find all positive integers $n$ such that $n^2 + 3n + 2$ is a perfect square.

第2题

从集合 $\{1, 2, 3, \ldots, 100\}$ 中选取两个不同的元素，使其和能被 $3$ 整除。求满足条件的选取方式总数。

From the set $\{1, 2, 3, \ldots, 100\}$, select two distinct elements such that their sum is divisible by $3$. Find the total number of valid selections.

第3题

设 $ABCD$ 为边长为 $1$ 的正方形，$PQRS$ 为其内接正方形，且顶点 $P$、$Q$、$R$、$S$ 分别位于边 $AB$、$BC$、$CD$、$DA$ 上。记 $\theta$ 为直线 $PQ$ 与直线 $AB$ 所成的锐角，求正方形 $PQRS$ 的面积。

Let $ABCD$ be a square of side length $1$, and let $PQRS$ be an inscribed square with vertices $P$, $Q$, $R$, $S$ lying on sides $AB$, $BC$, $CD$, $DA$ respectively. Let $\theta$ be the acute angle between line $PQ$ and line $AB$. Find the area of square $PQRS$.

第4题

设 $\Gamma_1$、$\Gamma_2$ 为两个半径均为 $1$ 的圆，圆心分别为 $O_1$、$O_2$，两圆相交于点 $A$。已知 $\angle O_1AO_2 = \theta$（$0^\circ < \theta < 180^\circ$），求线段 $O_1O_2$ 的长度。

Let $\Gamma_1$ and $\Gamma_2$ be two circles of radius $1$ with centers $O_1$ and $O_2$ respectively, intersecting at point $A$. Given $\angle O_1AO_2 = \theta$ ($0^\circ < \theta < 180^\circ$), find the length of segment $O_1O_2$.

第5题

设 $n$ 为正整数。

Let $n$ be a positive integer.

(a) 记 $E_n = \{2, 4, 6, \ldots, 2n\}$。令 $S_E = \sum_{k \in E_n} k$，$P_E = \prod_{k \in E_n} k$。求 $\gcd(S_E, P_E)$ 的最大值，或证明其可以任意大。

(a) Let $E_n = \{2, 4, 6, \ldots, 2n\}$. Define $S_E = \sum_{k \in E_n} k$ and $P_E = \prod_{k \in E_n} k$. Find the maximum value of $\gcd(S_E, P_E)$, or prove that it can be arbitrarily large.

(b) 记 $O_n = \{1, 3, 5, \ldots, 2n-1\}$。令 $S_O = \sum_{k \in O_n} k$，$P_O = \prod_{k \in O_n} k$。求 $\gcd(S_O, P_O)$ 的最大值，或证明其可以任意大。

(b) Let $O_n = \{1, 3, 5, \ldots, 2n-1\}$. Define $S_O = \sum_{k \in O_n} k$ and $P_O = \prod_{k \in O_n} k$. Find the maximum value of $\gcd(S_O, P_O)$, or prove that it can be arbitrarily large.

评分标准：每问15分，其中10分看正确性与连贯性，5分看解法的优越性。

Scoring: 15 points per sub-problem. 10 points for correctness and coherence, 5 points for elegance of the solution.

比赛介绍

ABOUT THE COMPETITION

比赛的由来

Origin

灵息杯，系由学生自发组织的数学竞赛。名称来源于创始人对数学的一个理解：充满灵气。解题时那个非同凡响的变换，巧妙的不等式放缩，第六感推动的数形结合……一一如此。我自认为做数学题做得不错，但却未达到神的境界，或许数学也只是凡人的游戏，不管怎样，我想让这个比赛充满灵气和趣味，不管是题目还是选手。

The Aether Mathematics Open (AMO) is a student-organized mathematics competition. The name derives from the founder's understanding of mathematics: it is imbued with spirit. The extraordinary transformations in problem-solving, the ingenious relaxations of inequalities, the intuitive leaps in combining number and shape—all of these embody this spirit. The founder considers himself competent in mathematics, yet far from divine; perhaps mathematics is merely a game for mortals. Regardless, the aim is to make this competition spirited and engaging, for both the problems and the participants.

英文名 AMO，全称 Aether Mathematics Open，直译"以太数学公开赛"。我找不到一个词汇来承载灵息这样轻盈而跳脱的概念，在浩如烟海的词汇中发现了这个。以太是过时的，不过现在我们用它来形容无处不在，就像数学，就像灵。也许有一天我们也会过时，但我们的故事会像颂扬以太的诗歌一样永垂不朽。

The English name AMO stands for Aether Mathematics Open. No single word could capture the lightness and elusiveness of "lingxi" (spirit-breath); it was found among a vast sea of vocabulary. Aether is an obsolete concept, yet we now use it to describe something omnipresent—like mathematics, like spirit. Perhaps one day we too will become obsolete, but our story shall endure like the poetry that once celebrated aether.

举办的方式

Format

我们将在网络举办，不限年龄，不限国家，不限制队伍人数（建议单人即可，但我们无法保证这一点）。我们将有小测和正赛。小测可以直接在公众号后台私信答案，不要在评论区发。发布解析的同时会刊登每一题第一位做出题目的参赛者以及解题方式最漂亮的一位参赛者和其解题方式，当然也有前5名。正赛届时可私信公众号获得举办方邮箱，将答案发送即可。批卷完成后可得到答复。我们可能会在就读学校申请举办线下赛事，若有在桂林就读初中的读者可以注意一下。

The competition is held online, open to all ages and nationalities, with no restriction on team size (single participation is recommended, though not guaranteed). There will be quizzes and formal contests. For quizzes, answers may be submitted via private message to the official WeChat account; do not post in the comment section. Upon publication of the solutions, the first solver of each problem and the participant with the most elegant solution will be named, along with the top five overall. For formal contests, participants may obtain the organizing committee's email address via private message and submit their answers there; graded responses will be provided. Offline events may be organized at the founder's school; readers attending junior secondary schools in Guilin are advised to take note.

题目来源

Problem Sources

小测题目可能会引用优秀题目，正赛的题目会全部自主命题。小测的题质量要求不高，正赛一定会严格审校，尤其是必须保证至少三分之二的主流数学解题大模型无法解出。这是我们规避作弊的根源手段。在线下赛事中则不会有这个要求，符合其年级的竞赛题水平即可。

Quiz problems may include selected outstanding problems from other sources, while all formal contest problems will be original. The quality requirement for quizzes is modest; formal contests will undergo rigorous review, with the specific criterion that at least two-thirds of mainstream mathematical problem-solving models must be unable to solve them. This is our fundamental measure against cheating. Offline events will not be subject to this requirement, adhering instead to grade-appropriate competition standards.

关于组委会

About the Committee

很遗憾目前组委会只有创办者一人，不过我升入初中后可能会找一些配得上称为数学竞赛命题者的人来这里，同时我也有一些数学好的小学同学。我希望大多数人尊重这个比赛，不过肯定会有人提出此类问题。在此我可以说我的数学水平应该是高于很多社会人士的。按照我们的理念与招新要求来看，组委会成员显然可以胜任初等数学内一切的命题与改卷工作。

It is regrettable that the organizing committee currently consists solely of the founder. However, upon entering junior secondary school, the founder intends to recruit individuals worthy of the title "mathematics competition problem composer," along with several mathematically gifted primary school classmates. The founder hopes that most will respect this competition, though questions regarding competence are anticipated. It may be stated that the founder's mathematical level exceeds that of many members of society; according to the competition's philosophy and recruitment standards, committee members are fully capable of all problem composition and grading work within elementary mathematics.

组委会招新

Recruitment

招！仅面向桂林城区的中小学生，以便联系和组织活动。不过在正赛中获得前5的有机会成为特邀嘉宾，来小测环节命题。

Recruitment is open! Exclusively for primary and secondary school students in Guilin urban area, to facilitate coordination and organization. However, those placing in the top five of formal contests will have the opportunity to serve as guest composers for quiz problems.
